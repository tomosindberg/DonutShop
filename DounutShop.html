Seattle Donut Shops

This program shows the number of donuts to bake per hour and per day.

Instruction:
  1. Type in the location of the Donut Shop
  2. Type in the time the Donut Shop closes
  3. Enter the minimum number of customer each hour
  4. Enter the maximum number of customers each hour
  5. Enter the average amount of donuts a customer purchases
